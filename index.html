Meet a Product Coach

Moskovchenko Anastasiia

After bombing my first Yandex interview 8 years ago, I spent my career at Phillips, Zen, VK, Yandex, and Funcorp learning both sides of the hiring process—as a candidate and as a hiring manager screening 200+ applicants.

I discovered that most PM interview failures aren't about skills, but about storytelling, frameworks, and confidence. That's why I started coaching, and now I've trained 100+ product managers who've landed roles at every major tech company.

My unique perspective from both sides of the table gives candidates the insider edge they need to succeed.
